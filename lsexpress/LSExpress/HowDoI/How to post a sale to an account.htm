How to post a sale to an account

How to post a sale to a customer's account

  • To post a sale to a customer's account for billing later, add the customer to the sale and select Pay. Instead of choosing one of the methods, select the actions menu and Post to Customer.

  • Post to Customer posts the invoice into the customer's account without receiving payment. The sale will be finalized and the amount posted to the customer's account.

  • If the customer decides to pay into the account at the point of sale, see the process at How to pay into a customer's account at the POS.

How to view a customer's payment posted to the account

  1. Open Customers and select the customer you want to view. From the menu, click Ledger Entries. Here you can view the history of transactions that have been posted for the selected record.

  2. From the Customer Ledger Entries, click Apply Entries from the menu to view the ledger entries that have been applied to this record.

How to see customer's balance

  1. From the Customer Card, you can see the Balance and Balance Due.

  2. Click the amount Balance.

    Balance specifies the payment amount that the customer owes for completed sales. This value is also known as the customer's balance.

    Balance Due specifies payments from the customer that are overdue per today's date.

     

  3. The red color Invoice means the amount is overdue balance. If you sum up, you will get the total Balance Due. However, if you sum up all the invoice, you will get the total Balance.
