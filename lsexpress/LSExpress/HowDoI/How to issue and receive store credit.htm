How to issue and receive store credit

For more information on how to set up a store credit, click here.

How to issue store credit

  • If the balance of the sale is negative, a store credit can be issued. Select Payment Methods and then Voucher.

  • The sale posts as store credit, and a receipt should be printed/emailed for the customer, as it has the store credit number printed on it that the customer needs to use it.

How to receive store credit

  • Suppose a customer has store credit and wants to use it to pay for purchases; open Payment Methods and select Voucher. The POS will then ask for the store credit (document) number.

  • Once the document number has been entered, the store credit payment is applied to the sale, and the store credit number cannot be used again.
