Close out at End of Day using the wizard

  1. Select the Close Out button from the Periodic Activities menu group. The system will create a new Close Out summary or select the newest one if any available summaries exist.

  2. Select a Cash Drawer and/or Staff criteria. Leave the fields blank to close out all drawers and staff members in one summary.

  3. Enter the counted amount(s) for each payment method. Click the three dots to enter the amount by denominations (if available). A red amount indicates that there is a difference between the counted and the calculated amount.

  4. Verify the counted amounts, and click Next to post the result.

  5. Finally, when you click the Finish button, the wizard offers to take you to the posted summary window.
