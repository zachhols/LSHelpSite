Definitions, acronyms, and abbreviations

Term Description
KDS Kitchen Display System
KS Kitchen Server
DS Display Station
KOT Kitchen Order Ticket
Order/Transaction An order in Hospitality is also a transaction. An order of food items makes up a Hospitality transaction.
Restaurant/Store A restaurant in Hospitality is the same data table as a Store in Retail.
Expeditor A display station that shows the whole KOT and when bumped indicates that the order has been served to the guest.
Preparation Station A display station that shows ordered items as lines. The line can be started and bumped to indicate the progress of preparation of the item.
